## RES-001-TRAPPED-COLLAPSE - Trapped Person / Building Collapse / Confined Rescue

**Category:** Rescue/Fire/Medical/Police  
**Priority:** P1  
**Source basis:** NADMA disaster response, TLSM trauma, rescue safety principles  
**Retrieval tags:** trapped, collapsed building, landslide buried, cannot get out, confined space, lift stuck with medical issue

### Dispatcher objective
Identify exact victim location and hazards, prevent unsafe bystander rescue, dispatch fire/rescue and medical support.

### Critical questions
- Exact location and access?
- What happened?
- How many trapped?
- Are they conscious/breathing?
- Any fire, gas, electrical, water or unstable structure?
- Can you hear them?
- Is the area still moving/collapsing?

### Caller response script
Say: "Do not enter an unstable area. Move to safety and keep others back." If caller is trapped: "Stay as still as you can. Protect your mouth and nose from dust with cloth if available. Tap or call out only when you hear rescuers nearby if you need to save energy." If caller can safely communicate victim location, gather room, floor, side of building and last known position.

### Responder guidance
Dispatch Bomba/rescue, ambulance and police. Handoff: collapse type, number trapped, last known locations, victim contact, hazards, structural instability, access/staging point and need for specialist rescue. If multiple victims, switch to MCI mode.

### Do not
Do not encourage untrained rescue into unstable structures or confined spaces. Do not assume one victim only.
