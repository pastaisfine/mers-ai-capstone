## FIR-001-BUILDING-FIRE - Building Fire / Smoke / Persons Trapped

**Category:** Fire/Rescue/Medical  
**Priority:** P1 if persons trapped/smoke inhalation/large fire; P2 otherwise  
**Source basis:** UTM fire SOP, NADMA disaster response, Bomba response framing  
**Retrieval tags:** fire, smoke, burning building, trapped, alarm, explosion

### Dispatcher objective
Get people out safely, avoid smoke, notify fire/rescue, identify trapped persons and hazards, coordinate medical support.

### Critical questions
- Exact building name/address?
- What is burning?
- Which floor/unit?
- Is anyone trapped or missing?
- Is there smoke in stairwell?
- Any gas cylinders, chemicals or electricity risk?
- Is alarm activated?
- Where is the assembly point?

### Caller response script
Say: "Leave the building now if you can do so safely. Do not use lifts. Use stairs if clear." If smoke is present: "Stay low under the smoke and move toward the nearest safe exit. Close doors behind you if possible." If trapped: "Go to a room with a window if safe, close the door, block smoke with cloth if available, signal from the window and tell me your exact floor and unit."

If caller reports a small fire and is trained with extinguisher, local SOP may allow limited instruction only if safe. Otherwise, prioritize evacuation. Tell caller not to re-enter for belongings.

### Responder guidance
Dispatch Bomba; add ambulance for injuries/smoke inhalation and police for traffic/crowd control. Handoff: building, floor/unit, fire location, trapped/missing persons, smoke conditions, hazardous materials/gas/electrical risks, access points, hydrant/assembly point if known.

### Do not
Do not advise re-entry. Do not use lifts. Do not ask caller to fight fire unless local SOP permits and caller is safe/trained.
