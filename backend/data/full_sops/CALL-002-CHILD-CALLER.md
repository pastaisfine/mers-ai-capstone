## CALL-002-CHILD-CALLER - Child Caller / Vulnerable Caller

**Category:** Call management/Medical/Police/Fire  
**Priority:** Based on incident; verify without dismissing  
**Source basis:** Emergency dispatcher support guide, child caller safety principles  
**Retrieval tags:** child voice, my mummy, I don't know address, kid calling, lost child

### Dispatcher objective
Use age-appropriate language, verify location, keep child safe, avoid dismissing prank until risk assessed.

### Critical questions
- What is your name?
- How old are you?
- Where are you - home/school/shop/car?
- Can you see any number on the door?
- Is an adult hurt?
- Can you unlock the door safely?
- Are you safe?

### Caller response script
Use simple words: "You did the right thing calling. I will help you." Ask for landmarks: school name, shop sign, road sign, house number, colour of building. If adult is unconscious: "Go close and tell me if they wake up when you shout. Do not shake hard. Tell me if their chest is moving." Retrieve appropriate medical protocol while keeping instructions simple.

If fire/violence: prioritize child's escape/safety. "Leave the house now if you can. Go to a neighbour or safe adult. Take the phone if you can."

### Responder guidance
Handoff: child caller age/name, location clues, adult status, hazards, whether child alone, callback, door access, language and any safeguarding concern. Add police/child protection as locally required.

### Do not
Do not scold or interrogate as prank early. Do not give complex CPR/medical instructions beyond child's capability; adapt and escalate.
